Client delivery workflows let you run a professional, consistent service operation without burning time on repetitive admin. From onboarding a new client to sending weekly reports, automation ensures nothing falls through the cracks.

## Key Delivery Workflows

<Accordion title="Client Onboarding Automation">
  When a new client signs, the workflow automatically creates their project folder, sends welcome documents, sets up their CRM record, and books an onboarding call — all within minutes of the contract being signed or payment being received.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Weekly Report Generator">
  Pulls KPIs from your connected platforms on a schedule, summarizes performance with an AI-written narrative, and emails a clean, formatted report to the client every Monday morning. No spreadsheet-wrangling required.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Project Update Notifier">
  When a task is marked complete in your project management tool — Notion, Asana, or ClickUp — the workflow sends the client a plain-language update via Slack or email. Clients stay informed without you writing a single status message.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Invoice and Payment Reminder">
  Automatically generates invoices at the start of each billing period and sends payment reminders on a defined schedule. If a payment is overdue past a threshold, the workflow escalates with a more urgent follow-up and notifies you internally.
</Accordion>

## Client Onboarding Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Trigger">
    The workflow fires when a payment is confirmed in Stripe or a contract is signed in DocuSign — whichever event marks the official start of the engagement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create workspace">
    Use the Google Drive or Notion API to duplicate a template folder and rename it with the client's name and project type. All standard documents — briefs, SOPs, reporting templates — are included automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to CRM">
    Create a new contact and deal record in GoHighLevel. Apply the relevant service-type tag so the right automations and pipelines kick in from the start.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send welcome email">
    Use a Claude-powered email template to draft and send a personalized welcome message. The prompt inserts the client's name, project details, and next steps so every email feels custom-written.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Book onboarding call">
    Send the client a Calendly link for the onboarding session. You can include this directly in the welcome email or as a separate automated follow-up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Notify your team">
    Post a message in your internal Slack channel with the new client's name, service type, deal value, and a link to their CRM record so everyone is aligned from day one.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Pair your onboarding workflow with a client intake form built in Typeform or on your landing page. The form data populates every step — no copy-paste required.
</Tip>
