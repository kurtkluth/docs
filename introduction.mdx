## What's Inside the Community

Everything lives inside the free Skool group at [skool.com/ai-builders-circle-9648](https://www.skool.com/ai-builders-circle-9648). Here's a quick map of what you'll find once you're in:

- **Classroom** — Structured tutorial courses and session replays organized by tool and use case, so you can learn at your own pace.
- **Community Channels** — Four focused discussion channels: **General** for open conversation, **Ask for Help** for troubleshooting, **Wins + Results** for celebrating progress, and **Free Resources** for templates and tools shared by Josh and members.
- **Calendar** — Live sessions, office hours, and events you can attend in real time and ask questions directly.
- **Members Directory** — Browse and connect with other builders in the community by name, background, and what they're working on.
- **YouTube Library** — Full video walkthroughs of every major tool and workflow, hosted by Josh and linked throughout the knowledge base.
- **Free Workflow Templates** — Pre-built automation templates you can import directly into n8n or your tool of choice and customize in minutes.
