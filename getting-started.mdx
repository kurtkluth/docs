AI Builders Circle is a free community on Skool where you get access to tutorials, workflow templates, and a growing library of AI and automation resources. Whether you want to automate repetitive tasks, build AI-powered tools, or just learn what's possible with today's AI stack, everything you need is already here. Getting started takes about two minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the Community">
    Go to [skool.com/ai-builders-circle-9648](https://www.skool.com/ai-builders-circle-9648) and click **Join Group**. It's completely free. If you don't have a Skool account yet, you'll be prompted to create one — it only takes a moment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Introduce Yourself">
    Post an introduction in the **General** channel. Tell the community what you're building, what you want to automate, or what brought you here. This is the fastest way to connect with people working on the same things you are.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick Your First Resource">
    Head to the **Classroom** tab for structured courses and session replays that walk you through tools and workflows in order, or browse the **Free Resources** channel for plug-and-play templates you can deploy right away.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the Calendar">
    Open the **Calendar** tab to see upcoming live sessions and office hours. Attending a live event is one of the fastest ways to get your specific questions answered directly by Josh or other experienced builders in the group.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Building">
    Follow a tutorial, grab a template, and deploy your first workflow. Don't wait until everything is perfect — ship something small and iterate. The **Ask for Help** channel is always open if you get stuck anywhere along the way.
  </Step>
</Steps>
