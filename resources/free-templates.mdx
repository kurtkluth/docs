AI Builders Circle templates are plug-and-play starting points built and tested by Josh. Instead of building from scratch, you import the workflow, update your credentials, and customize from there — saving hours of setup time.

## How to Use a Template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the template you want">
    Templates are shared in the **Free Resources 🎁** channel in the community, and some are linked directly in YouTube video descriptions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download or copy the template">
    Download the JSON file (for n8n workflows) or copy the prompt text directly from the post.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import into n8n">
    In n8n, go to **Workflows → Import from File** and upload the JSON file you downloaded.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your credentials">
    Open the imported workflow and update each node's credentials to use your own accounts and API keys.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the workflow">
    Run the workflow using **Test Workflow** to verify it works correctly with your data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate when ready">
    Once you're satisfied with the results, activate the workflow so it runs on its trigger schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Getting Access

Templates are free and shared openly within the community. Here's where to find them:

- **Join the community** — Head to [skool.com/ai-builders-circle-9648](https://www.skool.com/ai-builders-circle-9648) and create your free account.
- **Check the Free Resources 🎁 channel** — This is the primary home for newly posted templates. Browse by post or search for a specific use case.
- **Watch Josh's YouTube videos** — Most tutorials include a downloadable template in the video description. Check the [YouTube Library](/resources/youtube-library) for a full list of walkthroughs.
- **Browse the Classroom** — Step-by-step courses inside the community Classroom walk through the most popular templates end to end.

<Note>
  Templates are shared as-is and built for specific use cases. You may need to adjust node configurations for your specific tools, accounts, and data structures.
</Note>
