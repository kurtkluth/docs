Here are answers to the most common questions from new and existing AI Builders Circle members. If your question isn't here, drop it in the **Ask for Help 🧠** channel and the community will get you sorted.

## Membership

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is AI Builders Circle really free?">
    Yes. The community on Skool is completely free to join. Some resources like courses or premium templates may have separate pricing, but the community itself — including the discussion channels, free templates, and YouTube resources — is free.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I join?">
    Go to [skool.com/ai-builders-circle-9648](https://www.skool.com/ai-builders-circle-9648) and click **Join Group**. You'll need a free Skool account to participate.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who runs this community?">
    AI Builders Circle is run by Josh Irwin, a builder and creator focused on AI, automation, and helping solopreneurs work smarter.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tools & Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which tool should I start with?">
    Start with **n8n** if your main goal is automation. Start with **Lovable** if you want to build an app. Start with **ChatGPT or Claude** if you just want to get better at prompting. The Guides section walks through each tool in detail.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need coding experience?">
    No. The tutorials in AI Builders Circle are designed for people with little to no coding background. Some advanced workflows involve reading JSON or basic scripts, but the walkthroughs explain every step so you're never left guessing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is n8n free?">
    n8n has a free cloud tier (with usage limits) and is fully free and open-source if you self-host. Most workflows in this community run comfortably on the free tier.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to pay for AI models (ChatGPT, Claude)?">
    You can start with the free tiers of both. For production workflows with high volume, a paid API plan is recommended. Claude Haiku and GPT-3.5 Turbo are the most cost-efficient options for automations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Templates & Resources

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where do I find templates?">
    In the **Free Resources 🎁** channel in the community, and in the description of Josh's YouTube videos. The [Templates](/resources/free-templates) page in this knowledge base lists all categories and explains how to import them.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I share my own templates?">
    Absolutely. Post them in the **Free Resources 🎁** channel. The community thrives on sharing — if you've built something useful, others will appreciate it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What format are n8n templates in?">
    JSON. You import them via **Workflows → Import from File** in n8n. Each template includes instructions for setting up credentials so you can get it running quickly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Getting Help

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I'm stuck on a workflow. Where do I ask?">
    Post in the **Ask for Help 🧠** channel. Include a description of what you're trying to do, what you've already tried, and any error messages you're seeing. The more context you give, the faster someone can help.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I find a bug in a template?">
    Report it in the community channel where the template was originally shared. Josh monitors community posts and updates templates regularly when issues are flagged.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Community Channels & Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What channels are available in the community?">
    Once you join, you'll have access to all the core channels and features inside the Skool community:

    - **General 💬** — Introductions, open discussion, and sharing what you're working on.
    - **Ask for Help 🧠** — Post your questions when you're stuck. Include what you've tried and any error messages for the fastest response.
    - **Wins + Results 🏆** — Share automations you've shipped, revenue you've generated, or time you've saved. Celebrating wins motivates the whole community.
    - **Free Resources 🎁** — Templates, prompt libraries, and downloadable configs posted by Josh and members.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the Classroom?">
    The Classroom is the course section inside the Skool community. Josh uses it to host structured, step-by-step courses on specific tools and workflows — going deeper than a single YouTube video. Access is included with your free membership.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I find upcoming events?">
    Check the **Calendar** inside the community. Josh posts live sessions, workshops, and Q&A calls there so you can plan ahead and join in real time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I find other members to connect with?">
    Use the **Members Directory** inside Skool to browse member profiles, see what tools and industries people are working in, and reach out directly. It's a great way to find collaborators or accountability partners.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where can I watch tutorial videos?">
    All major walkthroughs are on Josh's [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@JoshIrwin). The [YouTube Library](/resources/youtube-library) page in this knowledge base organizes videos by topic — n8n automation, AI agents, voice agents, and vibe coding.

</AccordionGroup>
